A short history of Garfields rise to fame

  • Before 1978 Garfield was on the rise to becoming a main character in his own comic strip
  • The Garfield Sunday strip was launched on June 25, 1978
  • The comic strip was available only at a third-page size until March 22, 1981
  • A half-page debuted the following Sunday, March 29
  • The Sunday strips for March 14 and 21, 1982, tried out a unique nine-panel format
  • The strip underwent stylistic changes, evolving from the style of the 1976â€“83 strips, to a more cartoonish look from 1984 onward
  • In 1981, less than three years after its nationwide launch, the strip appeared in 850 newspapers and accumulated over $15 million in merchandise
  • In 1982 the strip was appearing in more than 1,000 newspapers
  • By 2002, Garfield became the world's most syndicated strip, appearing in 2,570 newspapers with 263 million readers worldwide
  • By 2004, Garfield appeared in nearly 2,600 newspapers and sold from $750 million to $1 billion worth of merchandise in 111 countries
